Howdy,

Thank you for looking me up!

I got my ticket back in 2007.

I work from home and normally active on UHF/VHF 2m and 70cm on 9M2RKL or 9M2RKK, MARTS analog local repeater.

I am also working in digital mode, namely DMR, YSF and AllStarLink, which I think a much more reasonable option for my next step in this hobby. For DMR, I got a blue NanoPi Hotspot a MMDVM modem on RPi2, which I think much more stable and I can leave them running 24x7. My second MMDM in YSF mode running on RPi0 and the latest one is the AllStarLink node 608110, RPi2 connected to Baofeng 888 with ASL card by 9M2IBR.

Listening on TG91 and TG31482 South-Texas. Mostly active on TG50210 MalaysiaNet.

Hope to meet you on the air and and say "Apa khabar"
