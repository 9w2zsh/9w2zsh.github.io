## Looking for any good markdown editor

Moving forward, I would like to continue blog my personal and radio amateur activity on github.io. Therefore, I was looking for any good markdown editor hat is easy to use and will not cost much (free).

The idea is to be able to blog both on PC and tablet/mobile phone. Maybe will be mix of tools but the idea is to make sure that I will not be missing any good idea/point/event that is worth captured.

---
## List of editors

As usual Github is the best referal and people has shared lots of good stuff there. I found this repo by @mundimark, [Awesome Markdown Editors & (Pre)viewers](https://github.com/mundimark/awesome-markdown-editors)

I will start looking at them and the one that caught my eyes are:
1. [JekyllPad](https://www.jekyllpad.com/tools/online-markdown-wysiwyg-editor)
2. [StackEdit](https://stackedit.io/)
3. [Dillinger](https://dillinger.io/)
4. [Typo](https://typo.robino.dev/)
